The New completes and fulfills the Old
The Old foreshadows and points to the New.

The "old covenant"
defined sin and gave a way of righteousness through obedience.

It was not possible to keep all of the law but by faith in the Messiah redemption was secured.

The "new covenant"
was given by Jesus Christ and is fulfilled in Him.

Keeping the law was not possible in our own human strength. Salvation is now through faith in Christ Jesus as Lord and Savior.
